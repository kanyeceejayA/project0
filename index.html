About Ghost

Fortnite EGO is a military group that arrived first to Island Athena. It has members including Rippley, Cameo, Remedy, and 8-Ball. They arrived via a military plane, which crashed near the Shark's current location. Their logo can be seen in various locations around the map, including the Fortnite EGO outposts and a crashed plane on an island to the north of Sweaty Sands.
